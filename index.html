About Me

I am a Kenyan lady born and bread here. As a young girl I always wanted to be in the hospitality industry but sometimes life comes with its plate served and I had to join the technology world. My inspiration came from someone who started out so small but grew to be one of the best Software Developers in the country. I started watching youtube tutorials and in no time I could write my own application.

So far my area of expertise is working in the backend with Ruby On Rails and Python's flask framework. Before joining Microverse which is a remote school for software developers to either start or kick off your skills, I worked as an intern at Twiga Foods. I have greatly improved my skills with these two companies and also trying to help myself where I can with free learning facilities.
